[OpenNeuro](https://openneuro.org) is likely the oldest and the most well known [BRAIN Initiative](https://braininitiative.nih.gov/) archive for neuroimaging data. One of the exciting features of the project is that being inspired by our early [DataLad crawling](https://datasets.datalad.org/?dir=/openfmri) of its [OpenfMRI.org](https://openfmri.org/) precursor, it is now using [git-annex](https://git-annex.branchable.com) directly as its core technology. CON is contributing in ensuring availability of recent versions of git-annex and DataLad, involved standardization efforts, and inter-archive collaborations.
