[Open Brain Consent](http://open-brain-consent.readthedocs.org) initiative aims to facilitate neuroimaging data sharing by providing an "out of the box" solution addressing aforementioned human subjects concerns and consisting of:

- widely acceptable consent form allowing deposition of anonymized data to public data archives
- collection of tools/pipelines to help anonymization of neuroimaging data making it ready for sharing

Initiated and managed by CON, OBC is now an international effort with materials for GDPR compliance and multiple internationalization efforts to make sure materials are available in all applicable languages. [Contribute](https://github.com/con/open-brain-consent/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.rst) if you see your language is missing!
