[BIDS](http://bids.neuroimaging.io) is a project lead by a steering group elected by the BIDS community to provide a simple and intuitive way to organize and describe your neuroimaging and behavioral data.

## Publications and Resources

- Gorgolewski, K. J., et many, [Y.O. Halchenko](/whoweare#yaroslav_o_halchenko_) et many more (2016). [The brain imaging data structure, a format for organizing and describing outputs of neuroimaging experiments.](http://www.nature.com/articles/sdata201644) Scientific Data, 3. DOI: 10.1038/sdata.2016.44
- [Example (test) datasets](https://github.com/bids-standard/bids-examples)
- [Historical OpenfMRI](http://datasets.datalad.org/?dir=/openfmri) and up-to-date [OpenNeuro](http://datasets.datalad.org/?dir=/openneuro) Datasets in DataLad distribution.
