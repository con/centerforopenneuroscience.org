[ReproMan (Reproducible computational environments Manager; formerly known as REPROMAN)](https://github.com/repronim/niceman) is also a part of the NIH supported [ReproNim](http://repronim.org) effort. It aims to facilitate reproducible computation via collection of detailed information about origin of the used components (Debian and/or Conda packages, VCS repositories, etc), so that computational environments could be analyzed, and re-created.
