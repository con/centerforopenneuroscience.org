[Distributed Archives for Neurophysiology Data Integration (DANDI)](https://dandiarchive.org) is a platform for publishing, sharing, and processing neurophysiology data funded by the [BRAIN Initiative](https://braininitiative.nih.gov/). The platform is now available for data upload and distribution, and provides supplementary client tools to assist with introspection and organization of data following NWB standard.
