[PyMVPA](http://www.pymvpa.org) is a Python-based framework for neural decoding using multivariate pattern analysis. It affords both volume- and surface-based analyses using a wide variety of supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods, representational similarity analyses, searchlight analyses, hyperalignment of representational spaces, and model-based decoding and encoding. The software also can be used for neural data other than fMRI, including analysis of MEG and EEG data through spatio-temporo-frequency band searchlights and cross-modal EEG to fMRI trans-fusion. It also has been used for analyses on data unrelated to neuroscience, demonstrating its general utility. PyMVPA also serves as a repository for sample data sets (e.g., Haxby et al. 2001) that has found wide applicability for education, development of new algorithms, or new analyses and independent research reports.
