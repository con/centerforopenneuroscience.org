Dr. Haxby for many years directed the [Dartmouth Brain Imaging Center (DBIC)](https://www.dartmouth.edu/dbic/) and the [Center for Cognitive Neuroscience (CCN)](https://www.dartmouth.edu/ccn/) and cradled many of the CON projects. Dr. Haxby has an outstanding history of developing and disseminating in the open novel research methodologies, as well as influential datasets.
