Research Professor of Psychological and Brain Sciences and Adjunct Professor of Computer Science at Dartmouth, official Debian Developer, contributor to [many](https://www.openhub.net/accounts/yarikoptic) open source projects -- Yaroslav (AKA Yarik) is a lead of [DANDI](/projects/dandi/), [DataLad](/projects/datalad/), [PyMVPA](/projects/pymvpa/), [NeuroDebian](/projects/neurodebian/), and other [projects](/projects/). He is also currently serving on BIDS standard Steering and [NWB](/projects/nwb/) standard technical advisory committees, and engaged in various other initiatives, projects, and efforts.
