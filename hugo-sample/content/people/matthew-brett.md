Neurologist, neuroimaging methods guru, early proponent of reproducible practice and in-depth understanding of the used analysis methodologies, lead of [Neuroimaging in Python](http://www.nipy.org) community and some of its popular software ([NiPy](http://www.nipy.org/nipy) and [NiBabel](http://www.nipy.org/nibabel)) projects and infrastructural solutions, such as [Nibotmi](/projects/nibotmi/) which we are contributing to.
