Dr. Wager and his lab have a dedication toward developing analysis methods for functional neuroimaging and sharing ideas, tools, and scientific data with the scientific community and public. At the moment Tor is also leading [Dartmouth Brain Imaging Center (DBIC)](https://www.dartmouth.edu/dbic/) where CON pilots various deployments and automations to improve efficiency and reproducibility in the neuroimaging, and the [Center for Cognitive Neuroscience](https://www.dartmouth.edu/ccn/).
